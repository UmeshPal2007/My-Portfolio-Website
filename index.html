About Me:

I’m Umesh Pal, a BCA student at Amity University with a background in PCB (Physics, Chemistry & Biology) and a growing passion for software, AI, and data-driven problem solving. My journey into tech started with curiosity and turned into a hands-on pursuit of building practical solutions that create real-world impact. I’m especially interested in the intersection of AI, HealthTech, and analytics — where technology can simplify complex systems and make information more accessible for people. Over time, I’ve worked on projects focused on AI applications, data analysis, and intelligent systems, including:

AI Health Saathi — an AI-powered health companion designed to simplify medical reports and improve healthcare accessibility.
Trustline AI — a Generative AI-based news bias analyzer built to encourage more transparent and balanced information consumption
Aadhaar Enrollment Gap Intelligence Dashboard — a data-driven dashboard created using government datasets to analyze enrollment accessibility patterns.
Alongside projects, I’ve strengthened my practical knowledge through industry simulations, certifications, and virtual experience programs from organizations like Deloitte, Google Cloud, HP LIFE, McKinsey.org, Physics Wallah, and Internshala.

Skills & Programming Technologies: Java, Python, C, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Core Areas: AI Fundamentals, Prompt Engineering, Data Analytics, Problem Solving Tools & Interests: Generative AI, Dashboards, Data Visualization, HealthTech Innovation

Beyond coding, I enjoy learning in public and exploring how technology, education, and innovation can work together to solve meaningful problems. I believe growth comes from consistent experimentation, curiosity, and building things that actually help people.
Currently, I’m exploring opportunities in: Software Development AI & Data Analytics HealthTech & Emerging Technologies I’m always open to internships, collaborations, and opportunities where I can learn, contribute, and grow as a developer.
