About Me:

Hi, I'm

Umesh Pal

BCA Student | Tech Enthusiast | Problem Solver


I’m Umesh Pal, a BCA student at Amity University with a background in PCB (Physics, Chemistry & Biology) and a growing passion for software, AI, and data-driven problem solving. My journey into tech started with curiosity and turned into a hands-on pursuit of building practical solutions that create real-world impact.

I’m especially interested in the intersection of AI, HealthTech, and analytics — where technology can simplify complex systems and make information more accessible for people. Over time, I’ve worked on projects focused on AI applications, data analysis, and intelligent systems.

Beyond coding, I enjoy learning in public and exploring how technology, education, and innovation can work together to solve meaningful problems. I believe growth comes from consistent experimentation, curiosity, and building things that actually help people.

Currently, I’m exploring opportunities in: Software Development AI & Data Analytics HealthTech & Emerging Technologies I’m always open to internships, collaborations, and opportunities where I can learn, contribute, and grow as a developer.
